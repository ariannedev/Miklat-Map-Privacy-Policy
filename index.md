# Privacy Policy for Miklat Map

**Last Updated:** 15.01.2026

## Introduction

Arianne Burshtein ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Miklat Map mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

The App collects and uses your device's location data to:
- Display your current position on the map
- Find and display nearby bomb shelters
- Calculate distances to shelters
- Provide navigation assistance

**Location data is:**
- Collected only when the app is in use and you have granted permission
- Processed locally on your device
- Not transmitted to our servers or stored in the cloud
- Not shared with third parties (except map tile providers as described below)

### Settings and Preferences

The App stores your preferences locally on your device, including:
- Selected language (English, Hebrew, Arabic, or Russian)
- Selected default city
- Preferred unit system (metric/imperial)
- Disclaimer acceptance status

This data is:
- Stored locally on your device
- Not transmitted to any servers
- Deleted when you uninstall the app

### Device Information

The App may access:
- Device location services (GPS, network location)
- Internet connectivity (to download map tiles)
- Local storage (to cache map tiles and store settings)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide core functionality**: Display your location and nearby shelters on the map
- **Improve user experience**: Remember your language and city preferences
- **Display maps**: Download and cache map tiles from OpenStreetMap

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All user data (location, settings) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information.

### Map Tile Caching
Map tiles from OpenStreetMap are cached locally on your device for performance. These tiles do not contain personal information.

### Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect your data:
- All data processing happens locally on your device
- No user data is transmitted to external servers (except tile requests)
- Location data is only accessed when you grant permission

## Third-Party Services

### OpenStreetMap
The App uses map tiles from OpenStreetMap. When downloading tiles:
- Your device's IP address is sent to tile servers
- Tile requests may include general location area (not precise location)
- OpenStreetMap has its own privacy policy: https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

### Platform Services
The App uses platform-specific location services:
- **iOS**: Apple's Core Location framework
- **Android**: Google Play Services Location API

These services have their own privacy policies:
- Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell, trade, or rent** your personal information to third parties.

Location data is:
- **Not transmitted** to our servers
- **Not shared** with third parties
- **Not used** for advertising or analytics

The only external data transmission is:
- Map tile requests to OpenStreetMap (includes device IP address)
- Location permission requests to iOS/Android platform services

## Your Rights and Choices

### Location Permissions
You can control location access through:
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Miklat Map
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > Miklat Map > Permissions > Location

Options:
- Allow while using the app
- Ask every time
- Deny (app will not show your location)

### Data Deletion
To delete all app data:
- Uninstall the app from your device
- Or: Go to app Settings and clear all saved preferences

### Opt-Out
You can opt out of location collection by:
- Denying location permissions
- The app will still show shelters but not your location

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for emergency use and does not target children specifically. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Location data**: Not retained; processed in real-time only
- **Settings**: Retained until you clear settings or uninstall the app
- **Cached map tiles**: Retained until you clear app cache or uninstall

## International Data Transfers

The App is designed for use in Israel. Map tiles are downloaded from OpenStreetMap servers, which may be located internationally. Your location data is never transmitted internationally.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

This App provides information about bomb shelters for emergency situations. While we strive to provide accurate information:
- Users should always follow official emergency guidelines
- We are not responsible for the accuracy of shelter information
- In case of emergency, always call official emergency services:
  - Israel: 101 (Police), 100 (Emergency Medical Services)
