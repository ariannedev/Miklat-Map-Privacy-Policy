## Introduction

Arianne Burshtein ("I," "we," "our," or "us") operates the Bomb Shelter Locator mobile application, also known as **"Miklat Map"** (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the App.

> **Important:** This App is designed for use in Israel and is subject to Israeli privacy laws.

---

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

The App accesses your device's location data to:

- Display your current position on the map
- Find and display nearby bomb shelters
- Calculate distances to shelters
- Provide navigation assistance
- Update map positioning and distance calculations in real time

**How location data is handled:**

- Collected only when the app is actively running and you have granted permission
- Processed in real-time using temporary variables in device memory
- We do not intentionally store location data on the device or persist it beyond the active app session
- Not retained beyond the active app session under normal operation
- Not transmitted to our servers or stored in the cloud
- Not shared with third parties (except as required for map display - see [Third-Party Services](#third-party-services))

### Settings and Preferences

The App stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

- Selected language (English, Hebrew, Arabic, or Russian)
- Selected default city
- Default city usage preference (always use default city)
- Preferred unit system (metric/imperial)
- Disclaimer acceptance status

**This data is:**

- Stored locally on your device only
- Not transmitted to any servers
- Deleted when you uninstall the app

### Device Information

The App may access:

- Device location services (GPS, network location)
- Internet connectivity (to download map tiles)
- Local storage (to cache map tiles and store settings)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide core functionality:** Display your location and nearby shelters on the map
- **Calculate real-time information:** Determine distances and detect movement
- **Improve user experience:** Remember your language and city preferences
- **Display maps:** Download and cache map tiles from OpenStreetMap

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Processing Only

All user data (location, settings) is processed locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information.

### Location Data Handling

- Location coordinates are held temporarily in device memory while the app runs
- No location history is maintained
- Data is automatically cleared when the app is closed
- No persistent storage of location information occurs

### Map Tile Caching

Map tiles from OpenStreetMap are cached locally on your device for performance. These tiles:

- Are standard geographic images without personal information
- May be stored temporarily to reduce data usage
- Can be cleared by clearing the app cache

### Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your data:

- All data processing happens locally on your device
- No user data is transmitted to our servers
- Location data is used temporarily to provide app functionality and is not retained after use
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks are used

## Third-Party Services

### OpenStreetMap

The App uses map tiles from OpenStreetMap (tile.openstreetmap.org). When downloading map tiles:

- Your device's IP address is sent to OpenStreetMap tile servers
- Tile requests reveal the approximate geographic area you are viewing (which map tiles are needed)
- OpenStreetMap servers may log these requests according to their policies
- We do not control OpenStreetMap's data practices

**OpenStreetMap's privacy policies:**

- [Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)
- [Tile Usage Policy](https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/tiles/)

### Navigation Services

When you use the "Navigate" feature to get directions to a shelter, the App opens your chosen navigation app and passes the shelter's coordinates to it. This may involve:

**Google Maps:**
- Shelter coordinates are passed to Google Maps
- Google's privacy policy applies: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Apple Maps:**
- Shelter coordinates are passed to Apple Maps
- Apple's privacy policy applies: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

**Waze:**
- Shelter coordinates are passed to Waze
- Waze's privacy policy applies: [https://www.waze.com/legal/privacy](https://www.waze.com/legal/privacy)

> **Note:** Your current location may also be shared with these navigation apps if you grant them location permissions separately. We do not control how these third-party apps handle your data.

### Platform Services

The App uses platform-specific location services:

- **iOS:** Apple's Core Location framework
- **Android:** Google Play Services Location API

These services have their own privacy policies:

- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do not control how these platform providers handle location requests.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

**Your data is:**

- Not collected on our servers
- Not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services (except as described below)
- Not shared with advertisers, analytics companies, or data brokers
- Not used for advertising, marketing, or analytics

**External data transmission occurs only when:**

- Downloading map tiles from OpenStreetMap (includes device IP address and approximate viewing area)
- Requesting location permissions from iOS/Android platform services
- Using navigation features (shelter coordinates passed to Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze)

### Voluntary Shelter Reports

The App includes a feature to report shelter issues via email. If you choose to send a report:

- Your device's email client opens with a pre-filled message
- The message includes shelter information (name, address, coordinates)
- **Your location data is NOT included** in these reports
- Sending the email is entirely voluntary and user-initiated
- The report goes through your own email service to our support address
- We only receive information you choose to send

### No Analytics or Advertising

This App does not use:

- Analytics services
- Crash reporting tools
- Advertising frameworks or networks
- Tracking pixels or cookies
- User behavior monitoring

## Your Rights and Choices

### Location Permissions

You have complete control over location access:

**iOS Users:**

- Go to: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Miklat Map
- Options: "Never," "Ask Next Time Or When I Share," "While Using the App"

**Android Users:**

- Go to: Settings > Apps > Miklat Map > Permissions > Location
- Options: "Allow all the time," "Allow only while using the app," "Ask every time," "Don't allow"

**Effect of Denying Location:**

- The app will still display the map and all bomb shelters
- Your location will not be shown on the map
- Distance calculations will not be available
- Navigation features will not function

### Data Access and Deletion

Since we do not store your data on servers:

- There is no personal data to request or access
- Location data is automatically cleared when you close the app
- Settings data can be deleted by:
  - Uninstalling the app, OR
  - Clearing the app's data through device settings

### Data Portability

As no personal data is stored on our servers, there is no data to export or transfer.
